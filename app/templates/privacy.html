Privacy policy

As you may know, your private life - not our business. We do not collect any data other than the history of your games.

What we do not keep:

  1. We do not store your passwords in cleartext
  2. We do not store identification information (such as device ID, operating system and web browser).

What we store:

  1. We keep the entire history of your games, to display your progress and statitiki.
  2. We keep your email address, to be able to reset your password.
  3. We do not send you any messages without your consent.

What we share:

  1. We do not share any of your information to third parties.
  2. We give the public access to profile data (history of games, comments, favorites) anyone.

Authorization:

  1. For the Web client uses the authorization based on Cookies.
  2. Mobile client uses authorization based on tokens.
  3. A token is generated on the server side, completely random and does not contain any user data.
