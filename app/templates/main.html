World's Biggest Database
1,743,871 problems
We collected 6,090,352 games from professional tournaments and analyzed about 500,000,000 positions in search for blunders with one solution.
We spent over 31158 core-hours for finding them. It will took about 92 weeks on Intel Core i7 processor.
Unique Content
No one ever seen
All problems were generated using newly developed algorithms, ensuring you didn't seen them before. The lack of human factors allowed to collect a large number of positions as surprising and awesome.
Open and Free
Once and for all
This project is designed to be free and always will be, because this is what we believe in. All source code is available on Bitbucket and you can use it the way you want. We provide API to get access to our database for research and fun, but we also provide raw data we used for this service.
